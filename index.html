About us

What is Blackbird anyway?

Blackbird is in the historical Nielson Tower in Makati. This tower was part of the Nielson Airport, the very first commercial airport in the Philippines. Blackbird's success enables the history of its location intact. The restaurant's Art Deco style uniquely maintains the integrity of the building from the past.
